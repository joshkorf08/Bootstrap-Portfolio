About Me

My name is Joshua Everett Korf. I go by Josh for short. I am a 30 year old adult male, who was born and raised in Ann Arbor, Michigan. Where I spent twenty two years, before moving to Grand Rapids, Michigan, 7 years ago to pursue new creative outlets. After settling in GR, I met my current bandmates, Nolan Krebs, and Andrew Tamlyn. With Nolan on bass, Andrew on guitar and lead vocals we formed Heaters. Over the next six years we toured relentlessly across the US and Canada. After three years on touring heavy, we were given the opportunity to go on our first European tour. Covering 14 countries over 2 months. Now focusing my creative efforts on webdevelopment/ design, and evetually interior design. I look forward to hearing from you.
